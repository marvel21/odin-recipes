Oyakodon

What is Oyakodon?

Oyakodon, literally "parent-and-child donburi", is a Japanese rice bowl dish, in which chicken, egg, sliced scallion and other ingredients are simmered together in a sweet-salty sauce made with dashi, soy sauce, and mirin and served on a bed of steamed rice. The name of the dish is a poetic reflection of both chicken and egg being used in the dish.
